Our Story

📖

It started on a winter afternoon — 17th December 2023 — in a tuition class in Bankura, West Bengal. Two strangers who happened to sit close enough for the universe to whisper something into the air between them.

You had a way of existing that made everything feel softer. Your laugh was the kind that rearranges something inside you. Your presence — quiet, warm, magnetic — became the background music of my days.

I don't know if you noticed, but I noticed everything. The way you'd tuck your hair. The way your eyes lit when you understood something. The way you made ordinary days feel like they were worth writing about.

This isn't just a birthday page. This is a love letter to the story that began that December — a story still being written, one heartbeat at a time.
